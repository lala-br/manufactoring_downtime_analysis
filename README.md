# Manufacturing Downtime & Productivity Analysis (Excel)
### Overview
This project analyzes productivity and downtime data for a soft drink bottling production line. Acting as a Production Manager, I evaluated line efficiency, identified key downtime drivers, and analyzed operator performance to uncover actionable improvement opportunities.

---

## Business Problem
A soft drink bottling line was experiencing **low operational efficiency and frequent downtime**, with no clear understanding of:

- Which operators were underperforming  
- What factors caused the most downtime  
- Where improvement efforts should be prioritized

## Key Results
- Overall line efficiency measured at **64%**  
- **Top 5 downtime factors caused ~80% of total downtime**  
- Identified one operator with consistently lower efficiency  
- Found that **3 of the 5 main downtime drivers were operator-related**  

> Results-focused insights show tangible business impact.

## Business Recommendations
- Provide **machine adjustment training** for all operators  
- Deliver **targeted batch change training** for the underperforming operator  
- Strengthen **preventive maintenance** and inventory checks
